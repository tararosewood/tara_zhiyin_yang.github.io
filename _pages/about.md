## About Me

I am a PhD in Chemistry at UCLA in Prof. Richard Kaner’s group. My research focuses on energy storage materials, especially lithium-ion, sodium-ion, and zinc-ion batteries.

I work on electrode materials, electrolyte engineering, and interfacial chemistry for next-generation energy storage systems.

## Research Interests
- Battery materials (Li-ion, Na-ion, Zn-ion)
- Electrolytes
- Graphene
- Electrochemistry
